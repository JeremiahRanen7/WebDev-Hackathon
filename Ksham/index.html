About Us

Empowering Individuals with Disabilities

Globally, there are over 680 million people who are deaf, hard of hearing, mute, or blind. India alone is home to more than 80 million individuals belonging to these diverse disability groups.

Sadly, an estimated 90% of these individuals lack access to education and often face poverty. For these individuals, accessing essential information, education, and communication channels remains a significant challenge.
